What is iProc?

iProc is an Indonesian-made eProcurement platform designed to help companies comprehensively manage various aspects of procurement, including demand planning, contract management, inventory management, and asset monitoring and management. It utilizes technology to enhance operational efficiency, reduce costs, and ensure transparency and compliance with company policies.

The combination of iProc and Pengadaan.com will maximize the digital potential of procurement and accelerate the company’s digital transformation.

Value-Added Solutions from iProc

Cost and Time Savings

With various features integrated into one platform, iProc can accelerate the procurement workflow and help companies make strategic decisions based on accurate data to identify better cost-saving opportunities.

The Core Infrastructure for Procurement 4.0

iProc supports your journey towards Procurement 4.0, where every strategic and operational decision is made based on in-depth data analysis, creating efficiency, effectiveness, and sustainable competitive advantage.

Real-Time Visibility

iProc allows you to track and monitor every step of your procurement workflow in real-time, enabling you to identify spending trends, evaluate supplier effectiveness, and make strategic adjustments as needed.

Centralized Data and Documentation

Our platform serves as a centralized hub that stores all procurement-related data and documents. You can easily retrieve and access important information, ensuring accuracy, compliance, and more efficient audit processes.

Strategic Procurement

With iProc, the procurement function is no longer just an administrative task, but an essential part of the business strategy to drive growth and innovation.

Applying GRC (Governance, Risk, and Compliance Principles)

iProc helps ensure compliance with rules and regulations, risk management, and the maintenance of good governance in the procurement process.

Our Key Capabilities

Commodity Management

Grouping, data collection, and commodity analysis enable more informed and strategic decision-making, supporting procurement optimization and cost savings.

Vendor Vanagement

Manage the entire vendor management cycle, from onboarding to performance tracking—all within one platform..

Smart Purchasing

This feature facilitates efficient operational/tactical procurement through access to Private Catalogues and Public Catalogues, providing ease in acquiring the goods and services needed.

Smart Sourching

iProc simplifies strategic procurement management processes, from procurement strategy planning and preparation to execution, ensuring best-value sourcing is achieved..

Smart Contract

The entire contract management process—from drafting, execution, monitoring, evaluation, to closure—can be managed efficiently with this feature. Additionally, e-Sign and e-Stamp features make contract management even more practical.

Smart Invoice & Payment

This feature allows companies to monitor invoice and payment status in real-time with an integrated three-way matching system.
